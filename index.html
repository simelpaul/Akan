AKAN NAME GENERATOR

This website sole purpose is to provide naming service to children born in Ghana.

A child is named according to the day of the week they are born and their gender too.

All you need to do is to input your date of birth and submit for it to get you the day of the week you were born and the name set aside for either the male or female child born on that specific day.
